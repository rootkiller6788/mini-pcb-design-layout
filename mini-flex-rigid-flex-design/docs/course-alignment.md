## Key Cross-Cutting Themes
1. **Impedance Control**: All 9 schools cover TL theory — Wheeler/Cohn implemented
2. **Material Science**: PI/LCP trade-offs covered across MIT, Berkeley, ETH, TUM
3. **Mechanical Reliability**: Beam theory and fatigue covered across all ME programs
4. **IPC Standards**: Industry standards referenced throughout (IPC-2223, 6013, 2152, 4202-4204, 4562)
5. **Manufacturing**: Process considerations (adhesive flow, coverlay, stiffeners)
