## Key Topics Covered

1. **Transmission Line Theory**: Telegrapher's equations, Z0, gamma, VSWR
2. **Impedance Calculation**: Microstrip (IPC-2141A, Hammerstad, Wheeler), Stripline (Cohn), CPW (Wen)
3. **PCB Stackup Design**: Layer assignment, symmetry, manufacturability
4. **Via Modeling**: Parasitic extraction, stub resonance, backdrilling
5. **Signal Integrity**: Eye diagrams, jitter decomposition, BER, equalization
6. **Industry Standards**: PCIe, USB, DDR, HDMI, Ethernet compliance
7. **Advanced Topics**: 112G PAM4, rigid-flex, glass-weave effects, PSIJ
